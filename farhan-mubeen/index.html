About Farhan Mubeen

Farhan Mubeen is a self-driven creative professional with roots in Areekode, Malappuram district, Kerala. He founded KAKOKI Creative Co in 2024 with one clear mission: to give local Kerala businesses access to the same calibre of branding and design that large corporations enjoy — without the enterprise-level price tag.

With expertise spanning brand identity design, logo design, web design, UI/UX design, and graphic design, Farhan has led over 100 projects for clients ranging from small traders in Kondotty and Kizhisseri to growing startups across Malappuram and Kozhikode — as well as Gulf NRI businesses looking to establish a credible digital presence in Kerala.

His work is recognised for its clarity, attention to cultural nuance, and a genuine understanding of what makes a local Kerala audience trust a brand. He collaborates closely with co-founder Fathwima Huda K and a small team of designers and writers who share the same standards.
